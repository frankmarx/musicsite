Gus Marx, age 22, is a producer, singer, and songwriter from Connecticut. He began writing music in 2019 and released an EP a year later titled News. As a producer, Gus has collaborated with other artists, while releasing a stream of singles over the course of 2021 including Boss Man. With influences from Pop, Funk, Hip Hop, and Rock, using guitars, drums, keyboards, samples, a flute, and synthesizers, Gus records all his music at home.
